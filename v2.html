Frequently Asked Questions

How Does Clima Pest Guard Work?

    Clima Pest Guard uses advanced UV light technology to attract mosquitoes and flying pests. Once they get close, they are instantly eliminated by a powerful, safe electric grid. It works automatically without any chemicals or sticky messes.

Is Clima Pest Guard Safe For Kids and Pets?

    Absolutely. Safety is our priority. The device is 100% chemical-free and non-toxic. It features a durable protective outer casing that prevents children or curious pets from touching the internal electric grid.

Is It Wireless and Portable?

    Yes! Clima Pest Guard is USB rechargeable and completely portable. You can charge it up and take it anywhere—from your bedroom to the backyard, patio, or even on camping trips.

How Long Does The Battery Last?

    Clima Pest Guard is equipped with a high-capacity rechargeable battery that provides long-lasting protection on a single charge, making it perfect for overnight use or outdoor evenings.

Does It Make A Lot Of Noise?

    Not at all. The device operates silently to attract bugs. You might hear a quiet, satisfying "zap" when it catches a mosquito, but it won't disturb your sleep or conversations like loud fans do.

How Do I Clean It?

    Cleaning is effortless. The dead bugs are collected inside the unit. You can simply shake them out into a trash bin or use the included small brush to sweep the grid clean.

Can I Use It While I Sleep?

    Yes! It is perfect for bedrooms. The soft UV light acts as a gentle nightlight while it silently protects you from bites, ensuring you get a peaceful, itch-free sleep.

Does It require Installation?

    No, Clima Pest Guard requires zero installation. Simply turn it on and place it where you need protection. It comes ready to use right out of the box.

Where Can I Use Clima Pest Guard?

    You can use it virtually anywhere! It's great for indoors (kitchens, bedrooms, offices) and outdoors (patios, balconies, gardens, camping tents). It provides 360-degree protection wherever you are.

How Do I Charge It?

    Clima Pest Guard comes with a standard USB charging cable. You can plug it into any USB adapter, power bank, or laptop to charge it quickly and easily.
