Frequently Asked Questions

How does Clima Pest Guard work?
Clima Pest Guard uses advanced UV technology to attract mosquitoes and zaps them instantly with a 360° electric grid, keeping your space bug-free without any chemicals.
Is Clima Pest Guard safe to use around kids and pets?
Absolutely. It is 100% chemical-free and features a protective outer casing that prevents accidental contact with the grid, making it safe for families.
Is Clima Pest Guard portable?
Yes! It is completely wireless and USB rechargeable. You can easily take it from your bedroom to the patio, camping trips, or anywhere you need protection.
Does it make any noise?
Not at all. Clima Pest Guard operates silently, allowing you to sleep peacefully or enjoy outdoor conversations without any annoying buzzing sounds.
How do I clean the device?
Cleaning is effortless. The device is designed to be mess-free; simply shake out the debris or use the included small brush to clear the grid quickly.
Does it require batteries?
No, it comes with a built-in rechargeable battery and a USB charging cable, so you don't need to keep buying disposable batteries.
How long will it take for my order to arrive?
All orders will be shipped UPS within 48 hours after order confirmation. Please allow 5-7 business days for standard delivery. You will receive an email confirmation with your tracking number.
